Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I dance salsa in Tampa Bay?

Maloof Entertainment hosts six recurring monthly Latin nights: Classic Salsa Sundays at 809 Lounge Tampa (first Sunday, 3 PM), La Sala at Babe Coffee Lounge in Land O Lakes (third Friday, 7 PM), Quality Inn Latin Night in New Port Richey (second Saturday, 7:30 PM), Lutz Latin Nights at The Drinks at 9 Bar & Grill in Lutz (fourth Saturday, 7 PM), Salsa Sol Sessions at Babe Coffee Lounge (fourth Sunday, 3 PM, pa' la gente), and Arthur Murray Re-Grand Opening Series in Tampa (third Saturday, 9 PM). All recurring events include a complimentary beginner salsa lesson unless noted.

Do I need to know how to dance?

No experience required. Every single event starts with a beginner-friendly lesson covering basic steps and timing. Most people who attend have never danced before. You will be moving confidently within 30 minutes.

Do I need a dance partner?

No partner needed at any of our events. Most people attend solo or in groups of friends. During lessons we rotate partners so you practice with multiple people and meet the room naturally.

What time do the beginner lessons start?

La Sala at Babe Coffee Lounge (Third Friday, Land O Lakes) ladies styling Salsa class at 8:30 PM. Classic Salsa Sunday (First Sunday, Tampa) lesson at 3:00 PM.

Is parking available and is it free?

Yes, free or accessible parking at all venues. Babe Coffee Lounge has convenient parking on site. 809 Lounge has its own parking lot.

What is the dress code?

Smart Casual or Dressy Casual at most events. La Sala at Babe Coffee Lounge — come looking great and feeling yourself. Classic Salsa Sunday at 809 Lounge is relaxed and family-friendly. When in doubt, dress up a little — the crowd tends to look good.

What are the age requirements?

La Sala at Babe Coffee Lounge (Third Friday) is 21+. Classic Salsa Sunday at 809 Lounge is the exception — it is a daytime matinee from 3:00 PM to 7:00 PM and is family-friendly and all ages welcome.

Can I make table reservations or book a group?

Yes. Contact us directly at (813) 738-2444 or [email protected] for table reservations and VIP group packages at any event.

Do you have Latin events in Clearwater, Largo, Dunedin, or Safety Harbor?

Yes. Maloof Entertainment serves the entire Tampa Bay Latin community. Our recurring events in Land O Lakes (Pasco County), Tampa (Hillsborough County), and New Port Richey (Pasco County) draw guests from St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Largo, Dunedin, Safety Harbor, Tarpon Springs, Pinellas Park (Pinellas County); Spring Hill, Brooksville, Wesley Chapel (Pasco/Hernando); and Apollo Beach, Ruskin, Sarasota (South Hillsborough/Sarasota County). All events are within easy driving distance from anywhere in the region.
