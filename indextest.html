Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I dance salsa in Tampa Bay?

Maloof Entertainment hosts multiple monthly events across Tampa Bay. First Fridays at LBC Palm Harbor (Viernes Social with the live Àmbito Latin Band), First Sundays at 809 Lounge Tampa (Classic Salsa Sundays matinee), and more across Pinellas and Hillsborough counties. All events include a beginner lesson and welcome all skill levels.

Do I need to know how to dance?

No experience required. Every single event starts with a beginner-friendly lesson covering basic steps and timing. Most people who attend have never danced before. You will be moving confidently within 30 minutes.

Do I need a dance partner?

No partner needed at any of our events. Most people attend solo or in groups of friends. During lessons we rotate partners so you practice with multiple people and meet the room naturally.

What time do the beginner lessons start?

Lesson times by event: Viernes Social (First Friday, Palm Harbor) lesson at 9:00 PM. Classic Salsa Sunday (First Sunday, Tampa) lesson at 3:00 PM. Check individual event listings for the most up-to-date schedules.

Is parking available and is it free?

Yes, free or accessible parking at all venues. LBC Palm Harbor has a large dedicated lot. 809 Lounge has its own parking lot, and our downtown venues offer accessible local parking.

What is the dress code?

Smart Casual or Dressy Casual at most events. Classic Salsa Sunday at 809 Lounge is relaxed and family-friendly. When in doubt, dress up a little — the crowd tends to look good.

What are the age requirements?

Most evening events are 21+. Classic Salsa Sunday at 809 Lounge is the exception — it is a daytime matinee from 3:00 PM to 7:00 PM and is family-friendly and all ages welcome.

Can I make table reservations or book a group?

Yes. Contact us directly at (813) 738-2444 or [email protected] for table reservations and VIP group packages at any event.

Do you have Latin events in Clearwater, Largo, Dunedin, or Safety Harbor?

Yes. Maloof Entertainment serves the entire Pinellas County Latin community. Our events in Palm Harbor and Tampa draw guests from Clearwater, Largo, Dunedin, Safety Harbor, Tarpon Springs, Pinellas Park, and across the greater Tampa Bay area. LBC Palm Harbor is 10 minutes from Clearwater and Dunedin.
